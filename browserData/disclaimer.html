VVO — Vitis Vinifera Ontology Disclaimer and License

The Vitis Vinifera Ontology (VVO) is developed by Dr Edit Hlaszny PhD, and Dr Christopher Johann Muller rer. nat., as an independent scholarly project. VVO is not affiliated with any university, research institute, or commercial organization. The ontology represents the authors' sustained effort, spanning more than nine years of collaborative research, to create the first comprehensive, BFO-aligned knowledge representation for Vitis vinifera L., grounded in the peer-reviewed viticultural and plant biological literature.

VVO is currently in active development. Class definitions, hierarchical structure, and annotation content may be subject to revision as the ontology matures toward formal submission. Users are advised to verify information against the primary literature cited in the definition sources of each class.

The methodological approach and structural design of VVO have been informed by the authors' study of the publicly available knowledge organization practices of the Julius Kühn Institut — Federal Research Centre for Cultivated Plants (JKI), Quedlinburg, Germany. The JKI's systematic work served as a methodological reference point. However, VVO is an entirely independent scholarly work. No data, definitions, terminological content, or structural elements of any kind have been derived from, reproduced from, or based upon JKI resources.
